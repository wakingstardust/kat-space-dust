【MISSION BRIEFING】// INTRODUCTION

-- MESSAGE BEGINS --

Welcome Space Cadet
This is a CLASSIFIED, TOP SECRET mission briefing, for your eyes only.
Your mission, if you choose to accept it, is to retrieve the keycap set code named KAT_SPACE_DUST.
Space exploration is the pinnacle of mankind’s scientific and technological innovation; however it often springs from a universal child-like curiosity of the unfathomable heavens.
This keycap set was brought together by cosmic forces and a shared passion of the aesthetic of the limitless aether, for a peculiar community interested in the design of advanced instruments that are finely calibrated for optimal thocking (keebs).
The design of this keycap set aims to celebrate this playful spirit, whilst incorporating the intoxicating indigo hues of interstellar space dust. There is something eternally entrancing and enigmatic about the beauty of interstellar dust and nebulae, and we hope we have captured some of that magic in this set.
Your mission objective is to procure the modules that will enhance the specialised equipment at your command.
All the information you need to know is attached in the files below.
Good luck and Godspeed, Space Cadet

-- MESSAGE ENDS --
